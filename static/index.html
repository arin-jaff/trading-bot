TRUMPGPT

TrumpGPT Trading Bot

Autonomous prediction market bot. Scrapes Trump speeches, trains a Markov chain language model, runs Monte Carlo simulations to predict which words he'll say, and trades on Kalshi when it finds an edge.

Navigate: Pipeline to watch training live • Markets for Kalshi data • Predictions for model output • Trading to configure the bot • System for Pi hardware
